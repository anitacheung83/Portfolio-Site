League Statistic Bot

Preface

Initially, I developed the League Statistic Bot with the intention to improve communication with my boyfriend. One of the challenges we face was the timing of my calls, particularly when my boyfriend was engrossed in a game of League of Legend. Understandably, interrupting him during those moments would lead to frustration, and he humorously referred to himself as being "at war" in the game. Thus, I sought a solution that would allow me to determine if he was currently playing League. By implementing the League Statistic Bot, I can now ascertain whether my boyfriend is playing League or not. Which enables me to wait until after his game to reach out, ensuring our conversations are more enjoyable and uninterrupted.

Further Application

League Statistic Bot has become a helpful tool for my family, especially when it comes to my younger brother's gaming habits. He struggles with self-control and to manage his gaming time effectively. So, my parents started using the bot to keep track of his playtime and help him with time management. It's made a significant difference in his academic performance. With the bot's support, my parents can better guide him and find a balance between gaming and his studies. It's been a positive intervention that's created a more supportive environment for his growth and success.

Description

The League Statistic Bot is a Discord bot designed to fetch League of Legend game data using the Riot Games API. It utilizes Discord's slash commands feature to provide users with convenient ways to track summoner's games.

Users can check if a summoner is currently playing League of Legends by using the /active slash command.

Moreover, users can request the data for the summoner's past 10 games by using /past slash command.

Additionally, the bot generates two graphs thorugh matplotlib. The first graph displays the total time played by the summoner during the last week, allowing users to visualize their gaming activity over time. The second graph shows the total number of games played by the summoner during last week, providing insights into their gaming frequency.

Lastly, the bot generates a comprehensive weekly report that includes a list of games played by the summoner during the previous week and total time played graph and number of games played graph.

Modules/Library Used

Discord.py is used for receiving and responding user’s requests.
Matplotlib is used to generate graph from summoner’s game data.
Datetime is used for working with dates and times.
